The top 5 Tollywood movies

The ranking is given based on imdb rating

Mayabazar

Balarama promises Subhadra to get his daughter married to her son. But when the Pandavas loses their kingdom to the Kaurava's, Balarama breaks his promise.

Sita Ramam

An orphan soldier, Lieutenant Ram's life changes, after he gets a letter from a girl named Sita. He meets her and love blossoms between them. When he comes back to his camp in Kashmir,After he gets caught in jail, he sends a letter to Sita which won't reach her.

Mahanati

Biography of Savitri, an actress from South India movie industry, who ruled the industry for two decades during 50s and 60s.

Aa Naluguru

A man fights for his philosophy: that human relations are more important than money.

Baahubali 2

When Shiva, the son of Bahubali, learns about his heritage, he begins to look for answers. His story is juxtaposed with past events that unfolded in the Mahishmati Kingdom.
